# Customer Management Skill

## Overview
This skill provides comprehensive customer profile management functionality.

## Features
- Create new customer profiles with basic info
- View detailed customer records and history
- Edit customer information (contact, preferences, etc.)
- Delete customer accounts (with data retention options)
- Search and filter customers by various criteria
- Track customer relationship history

## Usage Scenarios
- Onboarding new clients
- Updating customer contact information
- Managing customer preferences and settings
- Searching for specific customers in the database
- Maintaining customer relationship records
